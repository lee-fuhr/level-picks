How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or time trials. Instead, our recommendations come from years of reading published expert reviews (from trade magazines, tool review sites, and YouTube channels that actually take levels apart) and synthesizing long-term feedback from real owners, both on forums and in person.
